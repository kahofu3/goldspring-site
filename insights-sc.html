为何内地客户选择在香港设立信托？

Why Mainland Chinese Clients Choose Hong Kong for Trusts

随着中国内地高净值人士持续增长，香港作为国际金融中心及中国唯一的普通法司法管辖区，成为内地家族及企业设立信托的首选地。金泉集团作为持牌信托或公司服务提供者，协助客户充分利用香港的独特优势，实现财富传承与资产保护。

As the number of ultra-high-net-worth individuals in Mainland China continues to grow, Hong Kong — as an international financial centre and China's only common law jurisdiction — has become the preferred destination for mainland families and enterprises to establish trusts. Gold Spring Group, as a licensed Trust or Company Service Provider, helps clients fully leverage Hong Kong's unique advantages for wealth succession and asset protection.

01

普通法体系保障

Common Law Protection

香港是中国境内唯一采用普通法的司法管辖区，为信托提供稳定、可预测的法律环境，国际投资者亦熟悉此法律框架。

Hong Kong is the only jurisdiction within China governed by common law, providing a stable and predictable legal environment for trusts that international investors are familiar with.

02

税务优势

Tax Advantages

香港无资本利得税、无遗产税、无继承税，仅对香港来源收入征税，且信托分配无预扣税，配合广泛的避免双重征税协定。

No capital gains tax, no estate tax, no inheritance tax, territorial taxation on Hong Kong-sourced income only, no withholding tax on trust distributions, plus access to an extensive network of double taxation agreements.

03

永续信托

Perpetual Trusts

香港允许设立永续信托，无强制继承规则，委托人可完全控制资产分配方式，确保财富按家族意愿长期传承。

Hong Kong permits perpetual trusts with no forced heirship rules, allowing settlors full control over asset distribution and ensuring wealth passes according to family wishes for generations.

04

资产保护

Asset Protection

信托在个人资产与商业资产之间建立清晰防火墙，保护家族财富免受潜在债权人及法律诉讼的影响。

Trusts create a clear firewall between personal and business assets, shielding family wealth from potential creditors and legal claims.

05

通往中国大陆的门户

Gateway to Mainland China

香港是中国与世界之间的主要桥梁，提供进入大湾区及亚洲各地机会的无与伦比的通道，在全球认可的法律框架内管理资产。

Hong Kong serves as the primary bridge between China and the world, offering unparalleled access to opportunities in the Greater Bay Area and across Asia within a globally recognised legal framework.

06

专业人才与基建

Professional Talent & Infrastructure

香港拥有世界级的金融、法律及会计专业人才，多语种团队精通粤语、英语及普通话，能有效服务内地客户的跨境需求。

Hong Kong boasts world-class financial, legal, and accounting professionals. Multilingual teams fluent in Cantonese, English, and Mandarin effectively serve mainland clients' cross-border needs.

家族财富传承

Family Wealth Succession

陈先生为内地成功企业家，拥有遍布中港两地的房地产及投资组合。面对家族传承规划，陈先生透过金泉集团在香港设立全权信托，将核心资产转入信托管理。信托架构不仅避免了内地强制继承规则的潜在影响，更确保资产按其意愿分配予三名子女，同时享受香港零遗产税的优势。

Mr. Chen, a successful mainland entrepreneur, held real estate and investment portfolios across China and Hong Kong. Facing succession planning challenges, he established a discretionary trust through Gold Spring Group in Hong Kong. The trust structure avoided the potential impact of mainland forced heirship rules, ensured assets were distributed to his three children according to his wishes, and benefited from Hong Kong's zero estate tax regime.

企业架构重组

Corporate Restructuring

李女士经营一家在深圳及香港均有业务的家族企业。为准备公司上市及引入外部投资者，金泉集团协助李女士透过香港信托架构持有上市公司股份，既保留了对企业的有效控制权，又建立了清晰的企业管治框架，最终成功在港交所完成首次公开招股。

Ms. Li operated a family business with operations in both Shenzhen and Hong Kong. To prepare for an IPO and attract external investors, Gold Spring Group helped Ms. Li hold listed company shares through a Hong Kong trust structure. This preserved effective control over the business while establishing a clear corporate governance framework, ultimately leading to a successful IPO on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.

跨境资产管理

Cross-Border Asset Management

王先生为科技行业创业者，持有数码资产、传统股票及海外房地产的多元化投资组合。金泉集团为王先生设计了一个整合信托方案，将分散于多个司法管辖区的资产统一管理，优化税务安排，并确保资金流动性以把握新的投资机会。

Mr. Wang, a tech entrepreneur, held a diversified portfolio including digital assets, traditional equities, and overseas real estate. Gold Spring Group designed an integrated trust solution that consolidated assets across multiple jurisdictions, optimised tax arrangements, and ensured liquidity to seize new investment opportunities.
