我們的服務流程

Our Process

服務流程

Our Process

1

初步諮詢

Initial Consultation

了解客戶需求、目標及現有資產狀況，提供初步建議。

Understand client needs, objectives, and existing asset positions. Provide preliminary recommendations.

2

方案設計

Solution Design

根據客戶具體情況，設計度身訂造的信託或企業架構方案。

Design bespoke trust or corporate structure solutions tailored to the client's specific circumstances.

3

合規審查

Compliance Review

進行客戶盡職調查（CDD）及反洗錢（AML）合規檢查，確保符合香港監管要求。

Conduct Customer Due Diligence (CDD) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance checks to ensure adherence to Hong Kong regulatory requirements.

4

架構設立

Structure Establishment

草擬信託契約或公司章程，完成法律文件簽署及資產轉移。

Draft trust deeds or corporate articles, complete legal document execution and asset transfer.

5

持續管理

Ongoing Administration

提供持續的信託管理、會計、稅務規劃及受益人聯絡服務。

Provide ongoing trust administration, accounting, tax planning, and beneficiary liaison services.

6

定期檢視

Periodic Review

定期檢視信託架構，因應法規變化及客戶需求調整方案。

Regularly review trust structures and adjust solutions in response to regulatory changes and evolving client needs.
