About Gold Spring Group

金泉集團控股有限公司為香港持牌信託或公司服務提供者，專注為高淨值人士、家族企業及跨國公司提供信託設立、企業服務及財富管理方案。我們與客戶管理層緊密合作，設計務實方案，確保資產安全、財富傳承及企業合規。

Gold Spring Group Holdings Limited is a licensed Trust or Company Service Provider in Hong Kong, specialising in trust formation, corporate services, and wealth management solutions for high-net-worth individuals, family enterprises, and multinational corporations. We work closely with clients to design pragmatic solutions ensuring asset security, wealth succession, and corporate compliance.
